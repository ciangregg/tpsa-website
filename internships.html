    Hamilton

    Run by the School of Maths in Trinity, this is a six week research project. The professor you must find to supervise the project does not have to be from the School of Maths, but the project topic must have sufficient mathematics content. The research project can be done alone, or in groups of two or three. Financial support starts from €675 per person, depending on the group size.

    University College Dublin

    The School of Physics has Summer Research Studentships, with a deadline of 4th April (2024). Some of these were research related and others were more outreach focused. There is pay of €400 per week. A CV and cover letter was required for the application.

    Oxford

    The Rudolf Peierls Centre for Theoretical Physics runs a summer internship. Applications for summer 2026 will open in January/February 2026 with a likely due date of mid March 2026. Applicants are asked to submit a one-page application and an academic reference letter. You must have the right to work in the UK to apply. Other groups within the Oxford department of physics also run similar internships.

    Cambridge

    Cambridge offers the Philippa Fawcett Internship Programme for students who identify as female or non-binary in mathematics who are in second year and above. An expression of interest must be completed by late February, with a final application in late March.

    Queen's University Belfast

    Queen's University Belfast offers a summer studentship in the Astrophysics Research Centre. It runs for eight weeks over summer and offers £2000. The information comes out around April, with the application due at the end of April. A transcript, cv and form form is required for the application. The Centre for Light-Matter Interactions has also run studentships in previous years due at the beginning of May. (Did not run in 2025)
